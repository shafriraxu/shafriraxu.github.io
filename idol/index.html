LeBron James

LeBron Raymone James (/ləˈbrɒn/; born December 30, 1984) is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Cleveland Cavaliers of the National Basketball Association (NBA).
He has started at the small forward and power forward positions. James has won two NBA championships (2012, 2013), four NBA Most Valuable Player Awards (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013), two NBA Finals MVP Awards (2012, 2013), two Olympic gold medals (2008, 2012), an NBA scoring title (2008), and the NBA Rookie of the Year Award (2004). He has also been selected to 11 NBA All-Star teams, 11 All-NBA teams, and six All-Defensive teams, and is the Cavaliers' all-time leading scorer.

MAYDAY

Mayday (Chinese: 五月天; pinyin: Wǔ Yuè Tiān; Pe̍h-ōe-jī: Gō͘-goe̍h-thiⁿ), is a Taiwanese alternative rock band that was formed in the late 1990s with five members, Ashin (A Shin) (vocalist), Monster (Guai Shou) and Stone (Shi Tou) (lead guitars), Masa (Ma-sha) (bass guitar) and Guan You (Guan-You) (drums). Formerly making music under the name of So Band, they came to be known as Mayday in 1997, with the name originating from Masa's online nickname.Mayday won the Golden Melody Award for Best Band in 2001, 2004, 2009 and 2012.They are known as "king of concert" in China.

Heading

Tracy Lamar McGrady, Jr. (born May 24, 1979) is a retired American professional basketball player in the National Basketball Association (NBA). He is a seven-time NBA All-Star, seven-time All-NBA selection, and a two-time NBA scoring champion (2003–2004). McGrady entered the NBA straight from high school after being selected in the 1997 NBA draft with the ninth overall pick in the first round by the Toronto Raptors. He played in the NBA for the Raptors, Orlando Magic, Houston Rockets, New York Knicks, Detroit Pistons, Atlanta Hawks, and San Antonio Spurs. He also played for the Qingdao Eagles of the Chinese Basketball Association. In 2013, he announced his retirement from the NBA. McGrady was ranked #75 on SLAM Magazine's "Top 75 Players of All-Time" in 2003.

堅強，堅持，堅韌。

認識他是在他轉會的時候，從騎士去熱火，從克里夫蘭到邁阿密，為了冠軍，為了榮耀。他身上背負著太多太多的罵名，也背負著太重的夢想和負擔。可我卻被這個“小皇帝”深深吸引，不是因為他的暴力美學，不是因為他天賦異禀，而是因為他敢於承擔，勇於面對。那時候我才15歲，我看不懂籃球卻喜歡看，喜歡看他在球場上為了夢想，為了證明自己所付出的汗水和努力。他給我的人生影響很大，讓我有勇氣面對高考，面對一切的失敗。勝利終究會來臨的，看自己付出了多少艱辛。永遠不抱怨，愛家庭，愛自己，愛夢想。

友情，樂觀，勇敢 。

從無名小卒到亞洲演唱會之王，從校園樂隊到第二個披頭士，16年來，五月天走過了太多的艱辛與困難。在2006年意外聽到五月天的歌後，開始被主唱所吸引，更深的了解後才知道這一支樂隊是多麼不容易。友情、堅持、樂觀，五月天教會我跌倒後該怎麼爬起來。“如果我這一桿，又沒辦法進球，就像我的生活，一直在出差錯，也許我這一生始終在追逐那顆九號球，別忘了是誰在愛我，別忘了，是誰在罩著我。”人生的路上不要忘了感恩，不要忘了愛自己，不要忘了不斷前行。

熱愛，承擔，感恩。

認識他的時候我已經錯過了他的巔峰，看到的不是35秒13分，不是帶領球隊的佼佼者，而是一個傷痕累累的男人在面對傷痛，面對壓力，面對無助。在火箭被過度用身體導致他有不可逆的傷痛，被交易，被嘲笑，被替代，被遺忘，到退役前都沒能拿到總冠軍戒指，最後一次的擦肩而過，應該離冠軍最近的一次了吧，而他只是個替補，可有可無的替補。感嘆時光，歲月不饒人，驕傲的時光終究會被沖淡，只是因為一份熱愛而在不斷堅持，身體和靈魂，總有一個在路上。
